Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 2026.

myListApp respects your privacy.

The app does not collect, track, or transmit personal information to the developer.

Your lists, items, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple’s system storage. If you choose to use Backup & Restore, the app saves a copy of your data to your iCloud Drive so you can restore it later. This backup is stored in your personal iCloud account and managed by Apple.

Your data only leaves your device when you explicitly choose to:

  • Back up your lists to iCloud Drive
  • Restore lists from an iCloud Drive backup
  • Share content using iOS features such as Messages, Mail, or Files

The app:

  • Does not use analytics
  • Does not use advertising
  • Does not track usage
  • Does not create user accounts
  • Does not send data to developer-controlled servers

Data Usage

myListApp does not collect or process user data outside of your device.

Any data you create (lists and items) remains on your device and is only shared if you choose to export or send it. If you enable iCloud Backup within the app, your lists are stored in your personal iCloud Drive using Apple’s secure iCloud infrastructure. This data is not accessible to the developer.
